What You'll Need

  • Butter
  • Shortening
  • Powdered Sugar
  • Milk
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Mixing Bowl
  • Mixer
  • Spatula or Spoon

Step 1

½ cup butter + ⅓ cup shortening — beat until creamy (~2 minutes).

Step 2

Gradually add 4 cups powdered sugar, 1 cup at a time, mixing on low speed.

Step 3

Add 3 tbsp milk + 1½ tsp vanilla and mix on medium until fluffy.

Step 4

Adjust texture: add more milk if too thick or more sugar if too thin.
